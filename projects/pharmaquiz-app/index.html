PharmaQuiz App

November 28, 2022 · Dallas Garriott, Matt Magee, Cole Sandlin, Colin Smith

I developed a cross-platform quiz app for pharmacy students as part of a team and in collaboration with Michael Peabody, senior manager at the National Association of Boards of Pharmacy. Our team chose to use React Native in order to fulfill the request for cross-platform compatibility. My contribution was implementing the back-end Cloud Firestore database responsible for storing user-generated question banks. I chose to use Cloud Firestore because of its real-time capabilities and scalability. This decision allowed the app to facilitate instant sharing of study materials while also accommodating any future growth.
