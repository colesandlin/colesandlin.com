I developed a cross-platform quiz app for pharmacy students as part of a team and in collaboration with the National Association of Boards of Pharmacy. Our team chose to use React Native to fulfill the client's request for cross-platform compatibility. My contribution was implementing the back-end Cloud Firestore database responsible for storing user-generated question banks. I chose to use Cloud Firestore because of its real-time capabilities and scalability. This decision allowed the app to facilitate instant sharing of study materials while also accommodating any future growth.
