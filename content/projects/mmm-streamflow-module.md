According to the Cambridge Dictionary, the term mudlarking is defined as “the activity of searching the mud near rivers trying to find valuable or interesting objects.” This happens to be a hobby of mine, so I decided to create a MagicMirror² module to help me determine when conditions are optimal for it. It uses Node.js and displays the current water level of the Ohio River at the user’s location. I utilized REST API calls to the United States Geological Survey to obtain real-time data.
